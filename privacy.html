Privacy Policy

for CieloTracker by Sadiom • Last updated: August 2025

Overview

Sadiom respects your privacy. CieloTracker does not create user accounts and does not directly collect contact information in-app. Your alert thresholds and preferences are stored locally on your device.

Advertising & Tracking (AdMob)

To keep CieloTracker free, the app uses Google AdMob to display ads. With your permission via Apple’s App Tracking Transparency (ATT), AdMob and its partners may collect identifiers (such as the device advertising identifier, “IDFA”) and product-interaction data (e.g., ad impressions/clicks) to provide personalized ads and measure performance. If you deny ATT, ads may still appear but are non-personalized and IDFA is not used.

Related policies: Google Privacy Policy · Apple Privacy

Data We Process

  • Device Identifier (IDFA) — only if you grant ATT; used by AdMob for third-party advertising and analytics.
  • Product Interaction — ad engagement/measurement via AdMob.
  • Weather Requests — ICAO codes you enter are sent to weather providers to fetch METAR/TAF data; these requests do not include personal data.
  • Support Email — if you email us, we receive your address and message solely to respond.

Your Choices

  • Manage tracking anytime in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.
  • Reset the advertising identifier in iOS settings and opt out of personalized ads where available.
  • Consent dialogs for regional requirements are handled with Google’s User Messaging Platform (UMP).

Sharing & Retention

  • Limited data is shared with Google AdMob and its partners to serve and measure ads. We do not sell personal data.
  • We do not store weather request history on our servers. Support emails are retained only as long as needed to respond.

Children

CieloTracker is intended for professional and general aviation users and is not directed to children under 13.

Ad Verification

To enable advertising through Google AdMob, we host an app-ads.txt file to verify app ownership. The primary location is /app-ads.txt.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, email dispatchskylabs@icloud.com.

CieloCheck Lite Privacy Policy

CieloCheck Lite processes all data on-device and does not collect personal information. For inquiries, contact dispatchskylabs@icloud.com.
