Privacy Policy

for CieloTracker by Sadiom • Last updated: January 2026

Overview

Sadiom respects your privacy. CieloTracker does not create user accounts, does not collect any personal data, and does not use any third-party advertising or analytics services that track you. Your alert thresholds, preferences, and all other app data are stored locally on your device only. The app makes direct requests to public weather services to fetch METAR/TAF data using ICAO codes you enter — these requests contain no personal information.

Data We Process

CieloTracker processes no personal data. The only network activity is sending ICAO codes (e.g., airport identifiers) to weather data providers to retrieve aviation weather information (METAR/TAF). These requests do not include any identifiers, location data beyond what you explicitly enter, or other personal information.

  • Weather Requests — ICAO codes you enter are sent to weather providers; no personal data is included or collected.
  • Support Email — if you email us, we receive your email address and message solely to respond.

Your Choices

Since no personal data is collected, there are no tracking settings to manage. You can continue using the app with full privacy — nothing is shared with third parties for advertising or profiling purposes.

Sharing & Retention

  • No data is shared with any third-party advertising or analytics providers.
  • Weather request history is never stored on our servers (or any servers we control).
  • Support emails are retained only as long as needed to respond and resolve your inquiry.

Children

CieloTracker is intended for professional and general aviation users and is not directed to children under 13.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, email dispatchskylabs@icloud.com.

CieloCheck Lite Privacy Policy

CieloCheck Lite processes all data on-device and does not collect personal information. For inquiries, contact dispatchskylabs@icloud.com.
