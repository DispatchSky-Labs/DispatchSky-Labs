Flight Dispatch Study

Flight Dispatch Study

Dispatch knowledge built for active recall.

A focused study system for aircraft-dispatch candidates and working dispatchers. It uses guided sessions, cloze-style recall, smart repetition, progress tracking, and optional audio and haptic feedback to make study more structured and efficient.

Problem
Aircraft-dispatch material is broad, technical, and difficult to retain through passive reading alone.
What I built
A multi-platform study product that organizes dispatch knowledge into focused recall sessions and helps users revisit difficult material.
My work
Product concept, aircraft-dispatch content structure, study logic, interface design, platform development, analytics, testing, App Store preparation, and ongoing development.
Traction
Available on the App Store with ongoing downloads and active study usage.

Sadiom Flow

Sadiom Flow

Operational information made easier to scan.

A live browser-based aviation operational-awareness product that presents flight activity, timing, traffic-management information, and changing operational conditions in a fast, readable workspace.

Problem
Operational information becomes harder to interpret when flights, timing, traffic-management information, and changing conditions are spread across multiple views.
What I built
A live web product designed to organize operational information and make developing conditions easier to recognize.
My work
Product concept, aviation logic, workflow design, interface design, data presentation, web development, testing, deployment, and ongoing maintenance.
Traction
A live web product used daily by real visitors.

CieloTracker

CieloTracker

Weather changes without constant manual checking.

An iOS aviation-weather product that monitors METAR and TAF information and alerts users when selected airport conditions change.

Problem
Aviation weather can change between manual checks, especially when several airports must be monitored.
What I built
A weather-monitoring product that organizes airport observations and forecasts and provides configurable notifications for meaningful changes.
My work
Product concept, aviation-weather logic, interface design, notification behavior, widgets, platform development, testing, App Store preparation, and ongoing development.
Traction
A publicly released aviation-weather product available through the App Store.

Why I build
