Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ Frenzy)

What is SkyNotify?

SkyNotify hurls real-time aviation weather updates (METARs, TAFs, SIGMETs) and crazy alerts at dispatchers and pilots. It’s for fun only—check FAA sources!

Where’s the weather data from?

Snagged from NOAA’s Aviation Weather Center and licensed APIs—total weather wizardry!

How often’s the weather updated?

METARs and TAFs flip every 30–60 minutes; alerts blast in real-time—keep connected!

Why’s data not loading?

Check your internet, location vibes, or shake the app. Email support@skynotify.com if it’s still dead!

Is SkyNotify FAA-approved?

Nope, it’s a wild informational toy, not FAA-certified. Hit 1800WXBRIEF for real stuff!

How do I set up alerts?

Hit Settings > Alerts, pick airports/routes, crank conditions (wind, visibility), and unleash push notifications!
