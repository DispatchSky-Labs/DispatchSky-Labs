Flight Dispatch Question Bank Support

Flight Dispatch Question Bank is a structured study app for dispatcher and aviation knowledge practice, with cloze-style recall, guided sessions, and optional community submissions.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I start studying?
    Open the app and begin a session from Home. The default flow supports tap-to-reveal and quick Got it / Missed responses.
  • How do cloze blanks work?
    Hidden segments are shown inline and revealed step-by-step. Multi-blank questions are supported.
  • Can I submit or suggest question fixes?
    Yes. Use the submission tools in-app. If signed in with Apple, submissions can be sent for moderation.
  • My sync looks stale. What should I do?
    Check internet connection, reopen the app, then try again. If it persists, contact support below with app version and screenshots.
  • Does the app force required updates?
    Required app updates may block access until the latest version is installed when version verification indicates an update is needed.

Aviation Disclaimer

Flight Dispatch Question Bank is for educational and training support only. It is not an operational dispatch authority. Always use official regulatory publications, carrier procedures, and approved operational systems for live operations.

CieloTracker Support

CieloTracker provides aviation weather monitoring (METARs and TAFs) with alerting tailored for dispatchers and pilots.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is CieloTracker?
    CieloTracker monitors METARs and TAFs and highlights conditions that meet your alert thresholds. It is for informational use only; always verify with official sources such as 1800WXBRIEF.
  • Where does weather data come from?
    Data is retrieved from NOAA’s Aviation Weather Center and compatible aviation weather APIs.
  • How often is weather updated?
    The app checks for new reports on issuance; background checks depend on iOS scheduling. Notifications require an active internet connection and permission.
  • How do I set up alerts?
    Enter ICAO identifiers in the app. Under Settings → Thresholds, set your own Ceiling (feet) and Visibility (miles).
  • Why isn’t data loading?
    Check connectivity and try a manual refresh. If issues persist, email dispatchskylabs@icloud.com.

Aviation Disclaimer

CieloTracker is intended for informational use only. Always consult official FAA/ICAO sources before making operational decisions. Sadiom assumes no responsibility for use of this app in flight planning or operations.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

Sadiom respects your privacy. Our apps do not sell personal data and do not use third-party advertising networks. App data such as study progress, preferences, and local settings is stored on-device.

For Flight Dispatch Question Bank, optional community features may use Sign in with Apple and network services to submit and sync content. For CieloTracker, network requests fetch public weather data for user-entered airport identifiers.

Data We Process

  • App Function Data — Study state, preferences, and app configuration needed for core functionality.
  • Optional Support Email — If you contact us, we use your email/message only to respond.
  • Optional Community Submissions — Submitted content and moderation metadata used to review and distribute shared questions.

Sharing & Retention

  • No personal data is shared for advertising or profiling.
  • Operational app data is retained only as needed for product functionality.
  • Support communications are retained only as needed to resolve inquiries.

Children

These apps are not directed to children under 13.

Contact

For support, privacy questions, or App Store review follow-up, email dispatchskylabs@icloud.com. Typical response time: 24–48 hours.
