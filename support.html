Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is CieloTracker?
    CieloTracker delivers real-time aviation weather updates (METARs, TAFs, SIGMETs) and customizable alerts for pilots and dispatchers. It’s for informational use only; always verify with official FAA sources like 1800WXBRIEF.
  • Where does the weather data come from?
    Data is sourced from NOAA’s Aviation Weather Center and licensed APIs for reliable updates.
  • How often is weather updated?
    METARs and TAFs refresh every 7–30 minutes, based on source issuance. Alerts are pushed in real-time with an active internet connection.
  • Why isn’t data loading?
    Ensure your internet connection is active. Refresh the app or email dispatchskylabs@icloud.com for assistance.
  • Is CieloTracker FAA-approved?
    No, CieloTracker is an informational tool, not FAA-certified. Use official sources like 1800WXBRIEF for flight decisions.
  • How do I set up alerts?
    In CieloTracker, go to Settings > Alerts, select airports or routes, and enable alerts. The app currently triggers alerts for ceilings below 700 feet and/or visibility below 2 statute miles, with low ceilings or visibility formatted in red for easy analysis. Future updates will allow user-editable thresholds. Ensure notifications are enabled in iPhone Settings > CieloTracker > Notifications.

Aviation Disclaimer

CieloTracker is for informational use only and not FAA-certified. Always verify weather with official sources like 1800WXBRIEF before flight decisions.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 14, 2025

DispatchSky-Labs, creator of CieloTracker, prioritizes your privacy and complies with GDPR, CCPA, and Apple’s guidelines. CieloTracker is designed to minimize data collection.

  • Data Collected: Email (only if you contact support).

  • Data Sharing: ICAO codes (airport identifiers) are sent to NOAA’s Aviation Weather Center API to retrieve weather data. No personal data is collected or shared.
  • Data Retention: No data is retained. Support emails are deleted after resolution.
  • User Rights: Request confirmation of no data retention via dispatchskylabs@icloud.com.
