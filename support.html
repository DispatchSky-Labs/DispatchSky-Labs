CieloTracker Support

Welcome to CieloTracker Support by DispatchSky-Labs. CieloTracker provides real-time aviation weather monitoring (METARs and TAFs) with alerting tailored for dispatchers and pilots.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is CieloTracker?
    CieloTracker monitors METARs and TAFs and highlights conditions that meet your alert thresholds. It is for informational use only; always verify with official sources such as 1800WXBRIEF.
  • Where does the weather data come from?
    Data is retrieved from NOAA’s Aviation Weather Center and compatible aviation weather APIs.
  • How often is weather updated?
    The app checks for new reports on issuance; background checks depend on iOS scheduling. Notifications require an active internet connection and permission.
  • How do I set up alerts?
    Enter ICAO identifiers in the app. Under Settings → Thresholds, set your own Ceiling (feet) and Visibility (miles). For example, entering 2000 ft is evaluated as 020 (hundreds of feet) against BKN/OVC layers. Alerts trigger when reported or forecast conditions fall below your thresholds.
  • Why isn’t data loading?
    Check connectivity and try a manual refresh. If issues persist, email dispatchskylabs@icloud.com.

Aviation Disclaimer

CieloTracker is not FAA-certified and must not be used as a sole source for operational decision-making. Always verify weather with official sources such as 1800WXBRIEF.

Privacy Policy Updated

Last Updated: August 2025

DispatchSky-Labs respects your privacy and follows Apple’s App Store guidelines and applicable laws (e.g., GDPR/CCPA). This policy explains what we collect, why, and your choices.

Summary

  • We do not create accounts and do not collect contact information in-app.
  • Advertising (AdMob): With your permission via Apple’s App Tracking Transparency (ATT), we (and Google AdMob) may use the device’s advertising identifier (IDFA) and product-interaction data to show personalized ads and measure performance.
  • If you deny ATT, ads may still appear but are non-personalized; the IDFA is not used.

Data We Collect

  • Device Identifier (IDFA) — only if you grant ATT permission. Used for third-party advertising and analytics through Google AdMob.
  • Product Interaction (e.g., ad impressions, clicks) — used for ad measurement and analytics via AdMob.
  • Support Email — if you contact us, we receive your email address and message content solely to respond to your request.
  • Weather Requests — ICAO codes you enter are transmitted to weather providers to fetch METAR/TAF data; these requests do not include personal data.

How We Use Data

  • To deliver and measure ads (AdMob) and keep the app free.
  • To operate the app (fetch weather) and improve reliability.
  • To respond to your support requests.

Tracking & Your Choices

  • On first launch (or later in Settings), you can choose whether to allow tracking. You can change this anytime in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.
  • You may also reset the advertising identifier in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising.
  • Consent for ad personalization and regional requirements is handled with Google’s User Messaging Platform (UMP).

Sharing

  • We share limited data with Google AdMob and its partners to serve and measure ads according to their policies.
  • We do not sell personal data.

Retention

  • We do not store weather request history on our servers.
  • Support emails are kept only as long as needed to resolve the request.

Children

CieloTracker is intended for professional and general aviation users and is not directed to children under 13.

Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may have rights to access, delete, object to, or restrict processing of your personal data. To make a request, email dispatchskylabs@icloud.com.
